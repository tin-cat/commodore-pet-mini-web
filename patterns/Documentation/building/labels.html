Final touch: Labels

There are two labels to print: One for the front plate on the Body shell and another for the Monitor box rear cap. Download the labels from the official repository:

Commodore PET Mini files
If you're printing the PDF file, be sure to set the scale to 100%. If you're printing the PNG file, use the 600dpi setting.
Cut the labels following the included guiding lines and glue them.
You can protect the labels by adding a clear cello tape layer on top.

You're done! You now have your very own Commore PET Mini in all its cuteness!
