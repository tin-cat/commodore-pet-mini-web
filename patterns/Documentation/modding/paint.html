Paint it

Most white 3D printing filaments are not completely opaque, and I'm not convinced the original Commodore PET was completely white. Also, simulate the yellowing of the plastic over time is a nice detail.

I didn't need to paint the black parts, they look black and opaque enough. But If you like to make your PET even more realistic, I recommend you to paint the white parts with spray paint.

  1. Sand the surfaces a little bit with a fine grain sand paper.
  2. Apply a thin layer of " target="supplies">primer spray and let it dry for 10 minutes.
  3. Apply two or three thin color layers each 10 minutes. The colors I found resemble better the "Computer from the 70's look" plus a little bit of yellowing are " target="supplies">Light Ivory with matte finish, like this one
